## Purpose
Browse and explore the complete collection of 70+ developer roadmaps from the developer-roadmap project, with detailed information about each role and learning path.

## What is a Developer Roadmap?

A developer roadmap is a visual guide showing:
- **Skills to learn** - Technologies and concepts
- **Learning sequence** - What to learn first
- **Prerequisites** - Foundation knowledge needed
- **Career progression** - From beginner to expert
- **Technology ecosystem** - Tools and frameworks
